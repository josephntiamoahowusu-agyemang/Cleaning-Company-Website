About CleanPro Services

Since 2010, we've been the trusted name in professional cleaning services across the region.

Our Story

CleanPro Services was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services that people can trust. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted partner for over 5,000 customers.

Our founders, passionate about cleanliness and customer service, built the company on principles of honesty, reliability, and exceptional quality. Today, we're proud to be one of the most highly-rated cleaning services in the region.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and eco-friendly cleaning services that exceed our customers' expectations, making their lives easier one clean space at a time.
